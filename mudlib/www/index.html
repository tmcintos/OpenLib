As the UltraLib mudlib develops, certain packages which should be easily portable to other mudlibs are being made available to the general public. These packages may be found via anonymous FTP at knapp38.res.iastate.edu
Programmers Wanted!
Dysfunctional Mud is currently the test site for a mudlib in pre-alpha development. Experienced LPC coders familiar with mudlib internals are always welcome to help out with the project. Please send email to mud@knapp38.res.iastate.edu if you would like to help out.
