As the UltraLib mudlib develops, certain packages which should be easily portable to other mudlibs are being made available to the general public. These packages may be found via anonymous FTP at dm.imaginary.com

Programmers Wanted!!

Dysfunctional Mud is currently the test site for a mudlib in pre-alpha development. Experienced LPC coders familiar with mudlib internals are always welcome to help out with the project. Please send email to mud@dm.imaginary.com if you would like to help out.
